# NovaTech Solutions — Company Knowledge Base

## 1. Company Overview

NovaTech Solutions was founded in **2011** in **Austin, Texas** by **Dr. Priya Sharma** and **Marcus Webb**.  
The company specializes in cloud infrastructure, AI-powered analytics, and enterprise software.  
As of 2024, NovaTech employs **4,800 people** across **12 offices** worldwide.  
Annual revenue for FY2023 was **$620 million**, up 18% from the previous year.  
The company went public on NASDAQ in **2019** under the ticker symbol **NVTK**.

## 2. Products & Services

### 2.1 NovaCloud Platform
- **Launch year:** 2014  
- **Description:** Multi-cloud orchestration platform supporting AWS, Azure, and GCP.  
- **Key features:** Auto-scaling, cost optimization, one-click deployment pipelines.  
- **Pricing:** Starts at **$299/month** for the Starter plan; Enterprise plan is custom-quoted.  
- **Uptime SLA:** 99.95%  
- **Current version:** v7.3.1 (released March 2024)

### 2.2 NovaAnalytics
- **Launch year:** 2017  
- **Description:** Real-time business intelligence dashboard with AI-driven forecasting.  
- **Supported data sources:** PostgreSQL, MySQL, Snowflake, BigQuery, Salesforce, HubSpot.  
- **Max dashboard widgets:** 200 per workspace  
- **Data refresh rate:** Every **15 minutes** on Standard; **real-time** on Enterprise.  
- **Pricing:** $149/user/month (Standard), $349/user/month (Enterprise)

### 2.3 NovaSec
- **Launch year:** 2020  
- **Description:** Zero-trust security suite for enterprise networks.  
- **Certifications:** SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, FedRAMP Moderate  
- **Threat detection response time:** Average **< 3 seconds**  
- **Pricing:** $18/endpoint/month

### 2.4 NovaEdge (IoT Platform)
- **Launch year:** 2022  
- **Description:** Edge computing platform for industrial IoT deployments.  
- **Max connected devices per cluster:** 50,000  
- **Supported protocols:** MQTT, OPC-UA, Modbus, HTTP/S  
- **Pricing:** $0.004 per device per day

## 6. Engineering & Infrastructure

- NovaTech's primary data centres are in **Northern Virginia**, **Frankfurt**, and **Singapore**.  
- The engineering team uses **Go** and **Rust** for backend services, **React** for all frontend products.  
- Internal CI/CD is built on a custom tool called **NovaPipeline**, capable of running **12,000 builds/day**.  
- The company open-sourced its distributed tracing library **NovaTrace** in 2022; it has **8,400 GitHub stars**.  
- Database infrastructure runs on a mix of **CockroachDB** (for global OLTP) and **ClickHouse** (for analytics).  
- Average on-call response time for P1 incidents: **< 8 minutes**.

## 7. HR & Culture Policies

### 7.1 Leave Policy
- **Annual leave:** 25 days/year for all employees (increases to 30 after 5 years of service)  
- **Sick leave:** Unlimited (self-certified up to 7 consecutive days; doctor's note required beyond that)  
- **Parental leave:** 26 weeks fully paid for all parents regardless of gender  
- **Sabbatical:** Available after 7 years of service; up to 3 months at 60% pay

### 7.2 Compensation & Benefits
- **Equity:** All full-time employees receive RSUs; standard cliff is **1 year**, full vesting over **4 years**.  
- **Health insurance:** 100% company-paid premium for employee and dependants in the USA.  
- **Learning budget:** $2,500/year per employee for courses, conferences, and books.  
- **Home office stipend:** $800 one-time setup allowance for remote employees.  
- **401(k) match:** 5% company match (USA); equivalent pension contribution in other countries.

### 7.3 Remote Work
- **Policy:** Hybrid-first. Employees are expected in the office **2 days per week**.  
- Fully remote roles are available for senior IC positions (Staff Engineer and above).  
- Remote employees must be located in a country where NovaTech has a legal entity.

## 8. Security & Compliance

- NovaTech is compliant with **GDPR**, **CCPA**, **HIPAA**, and **SOC 2 Type II**.  
- All customer data is encrypted at rest using **AES-256** and in transit using **TLS 1.3**.  
- Penetration testing is conducted **twice per year** by a third-party firm (most recent: Rapid7, March 2024).  
- Bug bounty program is hosted on **HackerOne**; maximum payout is **$50,000** for critical vulnerabilities.  
- Data retention policy: customer data is retained for **7 years** unless deletion is requested.

## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the minimum contract length for enterprise plans?**  
A: The minimum contract length for Enterprise plans is **12 months**. Multi-year discounts are available (10% for 2 years, 18% for 3 years).

**Q: Can I export my data if I cancel?**  
A: Yes. Customers can export all their data in CSV or JSON format within **90 days** of cancellation. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted.

**Q: Does NovaTech offer a free trial?**  
A: Yes. NovaCloud and NovaAnalytics both offer a **14-day free trial** with no credit card required. NovaSec offers a **30-day free trial**.

**Q: Where is customer data stored?**  
A: By default, data is stored in the customer's chosen region. Available regions: **US-East, US-West, EU-West (Frankfurt), AP-Southeast (Singapore)**.

**Q: What programming languages does the NovaCloud SDK support?**  
A: Python, Go, Java, Node.js, Ruby, PHP, and .NET (C#).
